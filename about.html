About us

Founded in India on 2017, Eastern Hemlock for Building Contracting CO LLC SOC was born out of a passion for elevating luxury design standards in the region. Over the past 8 years, we have grown from a small, dedicated team into a leading name in interior design, landscaping, and swimming pool construction.

Our journey has been driven by a relentless pursuit of excellence, blending craftsmanship and creativity in every project we undertake. Today, Eastern Hemlock for Building Contracting CO LLC SOC offers a full range of design and construction services, backed by a rich history of successful projects in Dubai and India. Each client and project we engage with contributes to our ongoing story, as we continue to redefine what's possible in the world of luxury living.
