About us

Founded in 2017, Eastern Hemlock Building Contracting LLC SOC was conceived with a singular ambition—to redefine the language of luxury living. From its origins in India to its presence in Dubai, the company has emerged as a symbol of refined design, precision craftsmanship, and uncompromising quality.

We do not simply design spaces—we curate experiences. Every project is a statement of elegance, where visionary concepts are brought to life through meticulous execution and an obsessive attention to detail. From bespoke interiors to sculpted landscapes and architectural water features, our work reflects a seamless fusion of artistry and engineering.

Eastern Hemlock delivers a complete design-build experience for a discerning clientele who expect nothing less than excellence. Our portfolio across the UAE and India stands as a testament to our ability to transform ambition into iconic, timeless environments.

At Eastern Hemlock, luxury is not an offering—it is a standard we continuously elevate.
